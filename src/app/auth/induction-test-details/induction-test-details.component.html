4. a scheduler for the vehicle

Safe loading of a vehicle is crucial in preventing injury to road users and damaging property. There are huge economic savings to the community if the delivery arrives at its destination intact.
The safe loading of vehicles is vitally important in preventing injury to people and damage to property. There are fiscal benefits to all if the load arrives at its destination intact and without damage.
Ramana transport provide all the necessary restraining equipment for each truck. It is responsibility of driving to keep record and maintenance of straps, chains, pallet jacks, pallet angles, tarpaulin, oversize sign, flags, flashing lights and other equipment. We would like to inform that all the restraining equipment is property of Raman transport, and should be kept in truck at all time. Any of this stuff should not be removed from the vehicle at any time. We recommend only use of straps and chains if necessary instead of ropes, as straps and chains have comparatively high tension. A load is required to withstand;

As 80% of its weight in forward direction
50% of its weight in rear and sideways
And 20% of its weight upwards

Another key points to remember while restraining are;

1. Please be aware that any item, heavy or light, need to be restrained at all time.
2. A tautliner’s curtains are not enough to hold items even in load restrain curtains, restrains are compulsory in Ramana transport because of the product/item safety.
3. When load is settled, after driving a little distance, always double check the load and webbing tension.
4. Lower the fraction is, higher the risk is of load movement
5. COR rules applied when carrying an item on the truck
6. An individual can face a fine of up to300, 000 and in jail up to 5 years, whether, a company can face fines up to 3,000,000 dollars.
Tips for good restraining


1. A load against the headboard is more secure and easy to restrain
2. A load should be placed in a way, that its weight should be evenly distributed on axles
3. If needed, distribute the load to keep sufficient weight on all axles
4. Identify unstable loads, and try to tie down as much as possible, always recheck the after time interval.
5. Anything item should not be placed straight on the tray. Items should be on the pallets or dunnage are required on tray.
6. Always use rubber mats or dunnage on the tray to keep the friction maximum.
7. Higher the angle, weaker will be the lashing effectiveness, so try to keep the angle as low as possible
8. In multi-layer sheets or product, dunnage should be placing in a straight line under the lashings, not randomly

When spending all day on road, concentrating and focusing on road, it takes very minor thing to distract a person. As a result, distractions are responsible for one in ten crash fatalities. When it comes to distractions, most of us think only about mobile phone using, however there are number of thing which can distract a driver. Some of the examples are;

1. Using mobile phone(texting, youtube, emails)
2. Child crying or opening seat belt
3. Carrying a dag in cab
4. Eating or drinking
5. Navigation(missing a turn, lane change directions)
6. Stereo(music change, finding favourite songs)
7. Talking on phone or passenger
8. Billboards/fancy advertising
9. Any distractions of road

Although in all of these, mobile phone is considered to be most dangerous as because of reason it is very intentional and unimportant according to the situation. Government laws also refrain drivers using mobile phones, while driving, so for the safety of our and people around us, avoid any intentional distractions. Even if the call or email is important, could be the one you waiting overwhelmingly, what you can do is, pull over where safe, handbrakes on, then answer or read and if necessary, reply. But Ramana Transport do not allow drivers to use mobile phone, or any distraction while driving, any driver caught doing so, would face disciplinary action.
